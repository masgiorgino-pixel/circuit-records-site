Release notes

CIRCUIT 01 delivers a raw and functional approach to fast-paced tekno, built around pressure, repetition and physical impact. Both tracks have been around online for years, becoming solid references over time. This release seals them on vinyl, giving proper weight to two early productions that deserved a physical format. Final vinyl copies are currently in the last stage of production and will be available within approximately two weeks, expected around mid-May.
