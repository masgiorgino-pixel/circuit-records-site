About

Circuit Records is an independent micro-label based in Milan, born from the Circuit Gang crew. Rooted in the free party scene, the project evolved through years of underground experiences, moving between illegal raves, clubs and alternative spaces.

The label focuses on functional, high-impact sound designed for physical environments and real systems. Each release reflects a direct approach: raw structures, fast tempos and a strong connection with the dancefloor.

Circuit Records is not driven by trends, but by context — made for heavy systems.
