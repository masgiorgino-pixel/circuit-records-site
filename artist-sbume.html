SBUME

Sbume operates within the underground rave scene since the late '90s. Founder of Circuit Gang (est. 2008), now evolved into Circuit Records. Direct involvement in free parties, sound systems and uncontrolled environments.

Started experimenting with synths in the late '90s, moving through rave systems and underground contexts, sharing consoles with key artists of the scene. The sound is mainly tekno and tribe, with strong excursions into drum & bass.
