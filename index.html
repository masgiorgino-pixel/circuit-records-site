Milan / 2026

We are an independent micro-label based in Milan, born from the Circuit Gang crew (active since 2008). We come from years of involvement in the free party scene, later evolving into underground club environments and alternative spaces. We decided to press our identity on vinyl with no compromises — functional sound designed made for heavy systems and appropriate environments.
